World Cup 2022

On November 20, one of the main events in football will start - the World Cup, which this time will be hosted by Qatar..

The process of selecting the host country of the world forum, preparation and the funds spent on it, formation of the statute and calendar of the tournament can be combined with the word "first time".

For the first time, a tournament of this level is being held in a Middle Eastern country. Due to the heat in Qatar, the matches will be played for the first time at the end of the year, instead of in the summer as usual.
